About Me

 

Welcome to my portfolio! From Carolina to California, I started out my career as a UX Designer for Blizzard Entertainment, a dream job come true, but later I found UX Research to be more of my passion. Why? I love listening to people and their stories, and how research can be so foundational to the creation of products that ultimately improves people's lives.

After Blizzard, I traveled cross-country to Atlanta, GA to work as a UX Researcher at The Home Depot, where I worked on their core e-commerce shopping platform, where I led both strategic and tactical studies using mixed methods, such as usability testing, card sorting, interviewing, survey design and more, where the compelling insights drive the strategy/design for valuable people-centered experiences. Then Mayvenn came along and I become their first UX Researcher to join the team. I've always been lucky enough to work with an incredibly passionate and talented, cross-functional, and multidisciplinary team of engineers, designers, researchers, project managers, various stakeholders, and more.
