About Me

 

Welcome to my portfolio! I started out my career as a UX Designer for Blizzard Entertainment, a dream job come true, but later I found UX Research to be more of my passion. Why? I love listening to people and their stories, and how research can be so foundational to the creation of products that ultimately improves people's lives.

So nowadays I'm currently working as UX Researcher at The Home Depot, working on e-commerce shopping platform, where you can find me leading both strategic and tactical studies using mixed methods, such as usability testing, card sorting, interviewing, survey design and more, where the compelling insights drive the strategy/design for valuable people-centered experiences. Everyday I am lucky enough to work with an incredibly passionate and talented, cross-functional, and multidisciplinary team of engineers, designers, researchers, project managers, various stakeholders, and more.
