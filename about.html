About Me

 

I’ve always been fascinated and curious by the human experience, continuing to ask questions about people. Introverted and inquisitive. Today, this important aspect of my personality powers my motivation and passion for user experience.

As a UX Researcher, My strengths lie in mixed methods when it comes to research, creating and executing a wide variety of UX research methods, such as usability testing, card sorting, interviewing, survey design and more. Insights from these methods help inform the design for valuable experiences that are user-centered. As a UX Designer, I'm able to engage with all of the touch points of the UX process of making a product, from generative and evaluative research, to concept ideation, wireframing, and prototyping. I’ve worked with incredibly passionate and talented people over the years, currently at Blizzard working with a great cross-functional multidisciplinary team of engineers, fellow designers, project managers, stakeholders, and more.

Outside of my professional life, I’m a former band geek who loves all kinds of music (yes, even Country), video games (think Candy Crush, Solitaire and Overwatch), hiking, sightseeing, the list goes on....
